Technical Implementation

Advanced AI Image Prompting Techniques

I've developed this systematic approach to AI image prompting that consistently produces high-quality, on-brief results. My method breaks down prompts into structured components that guide the AI toward specific visual outcomes. This structured approach allows me to create image series with consistent style while maintaining creative flexibility. I've found that detailed attention to environment, lighting, and technical specifications produces significantly better results than general descriptions. For client work, I maintain prompt libraries organized by style and purpose for efficient production of consistent imagery.

Strategic Prompt Structure for Midjourney

Basic Formula: [Subject Description] + [Environment/Setting] + [Lighting] + [Mood/Atmosphere] + [Composition] + [Style Reference] + [Technical Specifications]

Example - Professional Business Portrait: "Professional female CEO in her 40s with confident posture, modern minimalist executive office with city skyline visible through large windows, dramatic side lighting with soft fill, confident and approachable mood, three-quarter view professional portrait composition, style of Annie Leibovitz corporate photography, ultra detailed, 8K, photorealistic rendering"

Example - Product Photography: "Sleek black wireless headphones floating against minimalist light gray background, product photography with ring lighting setup creating subtle reflections on surface, commercial advertising mood, centered composition with strong focus on product details, style of high-end tech product photography, photorealistic, studio quality, 8K resolution"

Negative Prompts: "distorted proportions, blurry, amateur quality, oversaturated colors, unrealistic lighting, pixelated, low resolution, watermarks, text elements"

Style Consistency Techniques:

  • Use consistent stylistic descriptors across a series
  • Reference specific artists or photographers for cohesive looks
  • Maintain consistent lighting descriptions for a series
  • Use seed numbers when available to maintain subject consistency

AI Video Production Workflow

1. Pre-Production Planning

  • Define video objective, key message and target platform
  • Determine optimal aspect ratio (9:16 for Stories/TikTok, 1:1 for feed)
  • Create storyboard with key frames and transition points
  • Write script with timed sections (focus on first 3 seconds)

2. Pika Video Generation Settings

  • Resolution: 1080p (highest available)
  • FPS: 30 for smoother motion
  • Duration: 15-30 seconds optimized for platform
  • Style consistency: Use consistent style descriptors
  • Scene transitions: Implement natural flow between concepts
  • Motion quality: Specify "smooth camera movement" and "natural motion"

3. Post-Production Enhancements

  • Text overlay addition in After Effects
  • Color grading for brand consistency
  • Audio selection and synchronization
  • Opening frame optimization for attention
  • Call-to-action integration at optimal points
  • Platform-specific adaptations (captions, size cropping)

4. Performance Optimization

  • Compression settings by platform
  • First-frame attention optimization
  • Text timing based on average view duration
  • A/B testing for thumbnail/opener effectiveness

My AI video creation process combines strategic planning with technical execution across multiple tools. I start with clear objectives before generating base videos with Pika or Hailuo, carefully crafting prompts that produce cohesive scenes with proper motion quality. The post-production phase involves enhancing these videos with professional editing tools to add text overlays, optimize colors, and integrate brand elements. This hybrid approach leverages AI's ability to generate diverse visual content while ensuring professional quality through traditional editing techniques.

Multi-Platform Content Optimization

Platform-Specific Design Specifications

Instagram

  • Feed Posts: 1080 × 1080px (1:1 square)
  • Stories/Reels: 1080 × 1920px (9:16 vertical)
  • Carousel Posts: 1080 × 1080px (consistent across slides)
  • Color Profile: sRGB
  • Text Recommendations:
    • Max 125 characters for optimal visibility
    • Key message in first 2 lines
    • Brand watermark in bottom right

LinkedIn

  • Standard Posts: 1200 × 627px (1.91:1 ratio)
  • Document Ads: 1200 × 628px
  • Color Profile: sRGB
  • Text Recommendations:
    • Professional tone
    • More text-heavy than other platforms
    • Clear headers for scanning
    • Company logo integration

Twitter/X

  • Standard Posts: 1600 × 900px (16:9 ratio)
  • Text Overlay Guidelines:
    • Place important text centrally
    • Higher contrast for readability
    • Minimal text (1-2 lines max)

Print Materials

  • Resolution: 300 DPI minimum
  • Color Profile: CMYK
  • Bleed: 0.125" on all sides
  • Safe Zone: 0.25" from edges for essential elements
