PhySim: Don't Just Learn Physics, When You Experience It!

An easy online platform that changes how you learn physics with fun, interactive experiments.

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Three.js WebGL Responsive Design p5.js Physics Simulation Generative AI Educational Technology Scientific Visualization Interactive Web Applications 3D Modeling Simulation Education Generative AI Vibe Coding Design (UI/UX)

About This Project

PhySim is an innovative online platform to make learning physics better and easier. We use interactive simulations and virtual experiments. This means you can do physics experiments on your computer or phone, just like in a real lab, but from your home. PhySim helps you really understand physics, not just read about it.

We focused on making this platform simple to use, with clear visuals and smooth performance. We used object-oriented design (a way to build software cleanly) and even some AI help. This project shows how I use game development ideas to make learning engaging and easy to understand.

The Problem We Solved

Many students find physics hard because it's mostly about reading and theory. It's tough to imagine how things work in real life. We saw that students needed a better way to experience physics, not just memorize it. PhySim was made to fix this by giving you real-life like experiments right on your screen.

My Work & What I Did

I was part of the main team that created PhySim. I helped with making the platform, designing the content, and ensuring it's easy to use (UI/UX). My main goal was to make sure the platform looks good and works smoothly. My tasks included:

  • Building interactive simulations using p5.js and three.js for great visuals.
  • Making the user interface (what you see and click on) with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Using Vibe Coding to build the platform efficiently.
  • Working on adding Generative AI to make learning paths smarter.

Main Features

Fun Physics Experiments

  • Interactive simulations for main physics topics (like movement, electricity, heat).
  • Shows real-world examples and lets you interact with them.
  • Step-by-step guides for experiments with instant feedback.

Matches Your Syllabus

  • Lessons are designed to match school and college syllabi (Matric and Intermediate).
  • Ready-made lesson plans and quizzes for teachers.

Free & Paid Options

  • You can try some simulations for free.
  • Paid plan gives you full access to all simulations, quizzes, and tutor help.
  • Special prices for schools and tuition centers.

Use Anywhere, Anytime

  • Learn from home, school, or anywhere you have internet.
  • Works on computers, tablets, and mobile phones.
  • Always online and fast to load.

What's Next & What We're Working On

PhySim is always growing! Here's what we are currently doing and what we plan for the future:

  • Becoming a National Tool: We are working hard to make PhySim a top educational platform all over Pakistan.
  • Better Security & Speed: We are upgrading our programming languages and system to make PhySim more secure, faster, and work even better for more users.
